# Ottoman–Venetian Bridge: Digital Humanities Series

A multi-phase digital project exploring cultural, diplomatic, and economic interactions
between the Ottoman Empire and the Republic of Venice (15th–18th centuries).

Each version (v1, v2, v3…) represents a focused case study using digital humanities methods
such as TEI/XML encoding, archival research, and map-based storytelling.

## Versions
- **v1 – Anna Notaras & Byzantine–Venetian Cultural Bridge**  
  Migration and cultural exchange after the fall of Constantinople (1453).

- **v2 – Ottoman–Venetian Diplomatic Letters**  
  Archival correspondence and ambassadorial reports (Relazioni).

- **v3 – Trade Routes & Maritime Exchange**  
  Visual reconstruction of Adriatic–Levant trade connections.
